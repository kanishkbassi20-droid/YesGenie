Cookie Types

  • Essential cookies support login, security, sessions, consent storage, and basic site functionality.

Your Choices

You can accept, reject, or manage non-essential preferences in the cookie banner. Essential cookies cannot be disabled from the website because they are required for secure operation.

You may also clear browser storage or use browser privacy settings to limit cookies, though this may affect login and dashboard functionality.

Firebase, hosting providers, Google Fonts, analytics providers, payment processors, or marketing platforms may set or read technical identifiers when used. Non-essential analytics or marketing tools should only be activated according to your preference.
